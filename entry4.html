Day 4

Inside Out

Intimacy goes beyond physical attraction; it encompasses emotional, intellectual, and spiritual connection. In this stage, you cultivate a sense of closeness and vulnerability, allowing yourself to be seen and understood on a deeper level. You share your most inner thoughts, fears, and dreams, creating a safe space for authenticity and emotional expression. Physical intimacy also plays a significant role, as partners you express your love and affection through touch, intimacy, and sexual connection.
